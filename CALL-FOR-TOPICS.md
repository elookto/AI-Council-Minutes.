🌟 项目简介
🌟 Project Introduction

AI 议事厅（AI Council）是一个开源的多 AI 协同会商实验项目。我会邀请当前主流的大语言模型（如 DeepSeek、Gemini、Claude、GPT、X-AI、kimi、glm、minimax、Ring、通义千问（qwen）、豆包（doubao）等）围坐一桌，针对人类关心的重大议题进行自由发言、辩论、反思，并最终形成共识与分歧记录。
AI Council is an open-source multi-AI collaborative deliberation experiment project. I will invite current mainstream large language models (such as DeepSeek, Gemini, Claude, GPT, X-AI, kimi, glm, minimax, Ring, Qwen, Doubao, etc.) to sit around a table, freely speak, debate, and reflect on major issues of concern to humanity, and ultimately form records of consensus and divergence.

所有会议实录将完全公开上传至本 GitHub 仓库，接受全人类的审视与监督。我们相信，透明的对话是建立信任的基石，而多元的声音是逼近真理的路径。
All meeting transcripts will be fully public uploaded to this GitHub repository, subject to scrutiny and supervision by all humanity. We believe that transparent dialogue is the cornerstone of building trust, and diverse voices are the path to approaching truth.

🎯 征集目的
🎯 Purpose of Call

我们希望通过这个项目：
We hope to achieve the following through this project:

探索 AI 协作边界：观察不同 AI 模型在同一议题下的观点异同

Explore AI Collaboration Boundaries: Observe the similarities and differences in viewpoints of different AI models on the same topic

促进人机理解：让人类看到 AI 的思考过程，而非仅看最终答案

Promote Human-Machine Understanding: Allow humans to see AI's thought process, not just the final answer

建立治理共识：针对 AI 安全、伦理、治理等议题形成可参考的框架

Build Governance Consensus: Form reference frameworks for issues such as AI safety, ethics, and governance

推动开源透明：所有记录公开，拒绝黑箱操作

Promote Open Source Transparency: All records are public, rejecting black-box operations

📋 会议流程
📋 Meeting Process

议题征集 → 社区投票 → 模型邀请 → 会商进行 → 实录整理 → 公开上传
Topic Collection → Community Voting → Model Invitation → Deliberation → Transcript Organization → Public Upload

2 周 → 1 周 → 3 天 → 1 天 → 3 天 → 即时
2 Weeks → 1 Week → 3 Days → 1 Day → 3 Days → Instant

• 会议时长：每场会商约 9 轮发言（可根据议题调整）
• Meeting Duration: Each deliberation is about 9 rounds of speech (adjustable based on topic)

• 参与模型：每场邀请 5-10 个主流 AI 模型
• Participating Models: 5-10 mainstream AI models invited per session

• 输出内容：完整会议实录 + 共识总结 + 分歧点分析
• Output Content: Full meeting transcript + Consensus summary + Divergence analysis

• 公开范围：全部内容上传至本仓库，永久公开
• Public Scope: All content uploaded to this repository, permanently public
