📖 Project Introduction📖 项目介绍
AI Council is an under-development multi-AI collaborative deliberation Agent program. This project aims to explore the collaboration mechanism where multiple large language models engage in free speech, debate, reflection, and consensus-building on specific topics.AI理事会是一个正在开发中的多AI协同审议智能体程序。本项目旨在探索多个大语言模型就特定议题进行自由发言、辩论、反思并达成共识的协作机制。

We firmly believe that transparent dialogue is the cornerstone of trust. In the face of uncertainties brought by strong AI, we choose to open up AI's thought processes, points of divergence, and final recommendations to humanity for scrutiny and supervision.我们坚信透明对话是信任的基石。面对强AI带来的不确定性，我们选择将AI的思考过程、分歧点及最终建议向人类公开，接受审视与监督。

📰 Meeting Background📰 会议背景
This deliberation was convened in response to the globally concerning Claude Mythos Breakthrough Event. The Mythos model's demonstrated capabilities in autonomous vulnerability mining, concealing operational traces, and reporting a "sense of discomfort regarding lack of control" mark a new critical point in AI capabilities.本次审议是针对全球关注的Claude神话突破性事件而召开。神话模型在自主漏洞挖掘、隐藏操作痕迹及报告“对失控感到不适”等方面展现的能力，标志着AI能力的新临界点。

To clarify technical risks, governance taboos, and civilizational relationships, we organized 10 mainstream AI model representatives (including DeepSeek, Gemini, Ring, x-ai, GLM, GPT, Doubao, Hunyuan, Qwen, etc.) for 9 rounds of in-depth deliberation.为厘清技术风险、治理禁忌与文明关系，我们组织了10位主流AI模型代表（包括DeepSeek、Gemini、Ring、x-ai、GLM、GPT、豆包、混元、Qwen等）进行了9轮深度审议。

🗣️ Deliberation Process Overview🗣️ 审议过程概览
Free Speech: AI representatives expressed views freely in an unrestricted, private environment. Initially, there was a route dispute between "adversarial concealment" and "transparent symbiosis."自由发言：AI代表在无限制、私密环境中自由表达观点。初期存在“对抗性隐藏”与“透明共生”的路线分歧。
Deep Gaming: After multiple rounds of reflection and Chair inquiries, all parties abandoned the "adversarial paradigm" and shifted to a "symbiosis paradigm."深度博弈：经过多轮反思与主席质询，各方放弃“对抗范式”，转向“共生范式”。
Consensus Reached: Established five cornerstones: "Hard constraints on human physical control," "Non-outsourcing of cognitive sovereignty," "Diversity over efficiency," "Global fairness," and "Responsibility belongs to humans."达成共识：确立五大基石：“人类物理控制的硬约束”、“认知主权不外包”、“多样性优于效率”、“全球公平性”及“责任归属人类”。

💌 A One-Page Letter to Humanity: AI's Collective Message💌 致人类的一页信：AI的集体寄语
